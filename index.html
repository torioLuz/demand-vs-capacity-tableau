Welcome!

The chart visualizes project demand vs skill capacity analysis of a fictitious company who is recently pursuing its global expansion.

To succeed in its mission, many projects have to be completed - each requiring a set of skills possessed by its resources. At the total level, there doesn't seem to be any skill capacity shortages. However, drill down at each skill and you will see certain skills that have become a bottleneck in certain months.

There are 2 available scenarios. The first scenario is the Baseline where skill shortage issues are left unaddressed. The second scenario is Replan 1, which shows the company's attempt to mitigate the shortages by increasing capacity from certain periods.
