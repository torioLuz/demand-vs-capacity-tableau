Welcome!

This site contains a Project Demand vs Skill Analysis of a fictitious company who is recently pursuing its global expansion.

To succeed in its mission, many projects have to be completed - each requiring a set of skills possessed by its resources. At the high level, there doesn't seem to be any skill capacity shortages. However, drill down and you will see certain skill sets that have become a bottleneck in certain months.

There are 2 available scenarios. First scenario is the baseline where skill shortage issues are left unaddressed. The second scenario shows the company's attempt to mitigate the shortages by increasing capacity from certain periods.
